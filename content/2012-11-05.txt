Meeting with Natalya Solzhenitsyna
Vladimir Putin met with Natalya Solzhenitsyna, a public activist and head of the Solzhenitsyn Foundation.
November 5, 2012
19:30
Novo-Ogaryovo, Moscow Region
1 of 2
With Natalya Solzhenitsyna, a public activist and head of the Solzhenitsyn Foundation.
The meeting took place on the eve of the 50th anniversary of the publication in the Novy Mir literary journal of One Day in the Life of Ivan Denisovich by Alexander Solzhenitsyn, a novel that brought him international fame.
