Meeting with permanent members of the Security Council
Vladimir Putin held a meeting, via videoconference, with the permanent members of the Security Council.
October 20, 2023
12:00
The Kremlin, Moscow
1 of 2
Meeting with permanent members of the Security Council (via videoconference).
Taking part in the meeting were Federation Council Speaker Valentina Matviyenko
, State Duma Speaker Vyacheslav Volodin
, Deputy Chairman of the Security Council Dmitry Medvedev
, Chief of Staff of the Presidential Executive Office Anton Vaino
, Security Council Secretary Nikolai Patrushev
, Defence Minister Sergei Shoigu
, Special Presidential Representative for Environmental Protection, Ecology and Transport Sergei Ivanov
and Deputy Prime Minister Dmitry Chernyshenko
.
* * *
President of Russia Vladimir Putin: Colleagues, good afternoon.
Today, we will discuss efforts to introduce modern technology for ensuring national defence and security.
Mr Chernyshenko will make a report today. Invite him, please. After that, we will exchange views on the most topical items on the current agenda.
Mr Chernyshenko, go ahead, please.
