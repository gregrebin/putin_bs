Working meeting with Director of the Federal Agency for Scientific Organisations Mikhail Kotyukov
Vladimir Putin had a working meeting with Director of the Federal Agency for Scientific Organisations Mikhail Kotyukov to discuss the various matters related to the agency’s work.
March 11, 2014
19:30
Sochi
4 of 4
Meeting with Director of the Federal Agency for Scientific Organisations Mikhail Kotyukov.
In particular, the discussion examined financing for scientific organisations in 2014 and following years, modernising and upgrading laboratory research bases, cooperation between the Russian Academy of Sciences’ various institutes and universities, and issues of concern to young scientists.
